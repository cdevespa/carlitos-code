Hi, I'm a visual artist who loves to code

A little bit about me.

I live in the San Francisco Bay Area with my son, Iggy. I became interested in coding because I wanted to supplement my analog artistic practice. You can see my art here. I am fascinated with the digital medium and how easily it can connect people from all over the world. I want to extend an invitation to become part of my design portfolio.

The process of web design is one more avenue to disseminate my work and artistic vision. I want to learn to build websites from scratch just as I learned to make oil paint from its basic materials. I Love that I can use the same sensibilities it takes to make art and apply them to coding and designing elegant and original websites.

Efficient
Code

Ease of use and intuitive flow are the perfect support for engaging imagery. I code sites that are responsive, interactive and make sense. The easier it is to find your way around a site, the more users will want to investigate and engage with your content.

Design and Development

I follow a minimalist philosophy: give design elements all the space they need to present ideas with beautifully spare elegance. The form (code) and content (design) reflect importance, refinement and intelligence.

Personalized Design

The websites I design are Meticulously handcrafted, totally original and stand out from other websites. I believe that the best way to get and retain viewers to your site is to present beautiful, stunning images that support your concepts and goals.
